Matthew R. Smith, Esq.

Matthew was born at the San Diego Naval Hospital on February 19, 1986 while his father was stationed in the Pacific with the United States Navy and is the oldest of three siblings. When he was one year old his family returned to Hart, where he would eventually attend Spitler Elementary. Son to Tammy Brimmer and Mark Smith his parents attended Hart and Shelby Public Schools respectively and their families are both longstanding members of the Oceana County community. At the age of nine the family moved to Howard City, MI for Markâ€™s career. Mark and Tammy would later divorce, leaving the family with Tammy and her requiring government assistance to help with food and housing. Eventually, Matthew would graduate from Tri County Area Schools at the top of his class and attend the University of Michigan where he would obtain a Bachelor of Arts Degree in both Political Science and History. From there he would, via the University of Toledo College of Law, graduate from Georgetown University Law Center in Washington, D.C. Longing to return to his Western Michigan home, he is seeking your support in becoming the Oceana County Prosecutor.
